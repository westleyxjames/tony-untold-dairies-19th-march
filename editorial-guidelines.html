Editorial Guidelines

Our Standards for Quality Content

Our Editorial Mission

Untold Daires is committed to delivering content that meets high standards of accuracy, usefulness, and integrity. These editorial guidelines outline the principles and practices that govern our content creation, editing, and publishing processes.

Our mission is to provide practical insights on home improvements, career development, technology trends, and sustainable living. Every article we publish is created with our readers' needs and trust in mind.

Core Editorial Principles

Accuracy

Accuracy is the foundation of all our content. We are committed to getting the facts right and correcting errors promptly and transparently when they occur.

  • All factual claims are verified through credible sources
  • Statistics and data are current, accurate, and properly attributed
  • We verify information independently when possible
  • Technical information is reviewed by subject matter experts
  • Errors are corrected promptly with clear notifications

Independence

Our editorial decisions are made based on reader value, not commercial interests. We maintain clear separation between editorial content and advertising.

  • Editorial content is not influenced by advertisers
  • Sponsored content is clearly labeled
  • We do not accept payment for favorable coverage
  • Product recommendations are based on merit, not compensation

Transparency

We are transparent about our methods, sources, and potential conflicts of interest.

  • We clearly distinguish between information, analysis, and opinion
  • Sources are identified when possible
  • Potential conflicts of interest are disclosed
  • Our revenue model is publicly explained

Usefulness

We focus on practical, actionable information that readers can apply to their lives.

  • Content addresses real reader needs and questions
  • Information is presented clearly and accessibly
  • We avoid unnecessary jargon and complexity
  • Advice is practical and realistic

Content Standards

Research and Verification

All content undergoes thorough research and fact-checking:

  • Multiple sources are consulted for each topic
  • Claims are verified before publication
  • Technical information is reviewed by experts
  • Current data and statistics are used
  • Sources are evaluated for credibility and bias

Writing and Style

Our content is written clearly and professionally:

  • Clear, accessible language appropriate for general audiences
  • Well-organized structure with logical flow
  • Accurate grammar, spelling, and punctuation
  • Respectful, inclusive language
  • Balanced presentation of different perspectives

Editorial Review

All content undergoes editorial review before publication:

  • Fact-checking of claims and statistics
  • Review for clarity and organization
  • Verification of sources and attributions
  • Assessment of usefulness and relevance
  • Checking for potential bias or conflicts

Corrections and Updates

When errors occur, we correct them promptly and transparently:

  • Corrections are made as soon as errors are identified
  • Significant corrections include clear notification
  • Original error context is preserved when appropriate
  • We acknowledge mistakes forthrightly

To report an error, please contact us at contact@untolddaires.site

Sources and Attribution

We properly attribute information and ideas:

  • Sources are clearly identified and linked when available
  • Direct quotes are accurately represented
  • Ideas and information are properly credited
  • We do not plagiarize or misrepresent others' work
  • Anonymous sources are used only when necessary

Conflicts of Interest

We identify and address potential conflicts of interest:

  • Personal financial interests are disclosed
  • Business relationships relevant to coverage are disclosed
  • Writers with conflicts may be reassigned or disclose prominently
  • Affiliate relationships are clearly indicated

Diversity and Inclusion

We strive for inclusive, respectful content:

  • Language respects all individuals regardless of race, gender, religion, orientation, disability, or other characteristics
  • We seek diverse perspectives and sources
  • Content is accessible to readers with varying abilities
  • We avoid stereotypes and generalizations

Privacy and Sensitivity

We respect privacy and handle sensitive topics responsibly:

  • Personal information is published only with consent or clear public interest
  • Sensitive topics are handled with appropriate care
  • Graphic or disturbing content is labeled appropriately
  • Sources' privacy preferences are respected

Commercial Content

We clearly distinguish between editorial content and commercial material:

  • Sponsored content is clearly labeled as such
  • Advertising does not influence editorial decisions
  • Affiliate links are disclosed
  • Product recommendations are based on merit

For more information, see our How We Make Money page.

Reader Engagement

We value reader feedback and engagement:

  • Reader comments and questions are welcome
  • Feedback helps us improve content
  • Concerns are taken seriously and addressed
  • We maintain respectful dialogue with readers

Continuous Improvement

We regularly review and update our editorial practices:

  • Guidelines are reviewed periodically
  • We adapt to changing best practices
  • Staff receive ongoing training
  • Reader feedback informs improvements

Accountability

We take responsibility for our content:

  • Authors are accountable for their work
  • Editors ensure standards are met
  • Leadership supports editorial independence
  • We address concerns promptly and fairly
